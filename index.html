Who i am

I work on backend systems as the principal engineer at WELL Health. WELL is a HIPAA-compliant messaging platform that helps practices reach their patients. Previously, I worked as an iOS instructor for the Make School Summer Academy. I enjoy reading and staying up to date with trends in technology. In my free time I learn new technologies, write code, help others with code, bike, and take photos. Checkout my most recent app (I really need to update it), Zen Workouts.

What i do

At Bowdoin I worked on multiple machine learning projects, including Neural Networks that read handwriting and break CAPTCHAS, Particle Swarm Optimization problems, Genetic Algorithms, and others. My Neuroscience research experience includes an ERP/EEG study on Human subjects that I designed and ran to better understand the categorization process of ambiguous stimuli in the brain; and two preliminary studies to better understand the rapid effects of Sex Steroid Hormones on the visual system in fish.

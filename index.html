Who i am

I am a Senior Computer Science & Neuroscience Major at Bowdoin College and will complete my degrees this May 2015. I lead the IT Advisory Council that improves the technology experience on Bowdoin's campus. I like to read a lot and stay up to date with trends in technology. In my free time I learn new technologies, write code, help others with code, bike, and take photos.

What i do

Over the last few years I have worked on multiple machine learning projects, including Neural Networks that read handwriting and break CAPTCHAS, Particle Swarm Optimization problems, Genetic Algorithms, and others. My Neuroscience research experience includes an ERP/EEG study on Human subjects that I designed and ran to better understand the categorization process of ambiguous stimuli in the brain; and two preliminary studies to better understand the rapid effects of Sex Steroid Hormones on the visual system in fish.

My Search
